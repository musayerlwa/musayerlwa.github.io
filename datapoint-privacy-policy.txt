Modified Date: 1st December, 2024 

Welcome to Datapoint! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use the Datapoint app.

To provide you with a personalized and seamless experience, Datapoint collects the following information during account registration or use:

Name: To personalize your account.
Phone Number: For account verification and communication purposes.
Email Address: To provide updates, support, and account-related notifications.
2. How We Use Your Information
The information collected is used exclusively for:

Account Personalization: To tailor your experience within the app.
Communication: To notify you about app updates, transactions, or support requests.
Security: To verify your identity and protect your account.
3. Data Security
We take your security seriously and implement robust measures to protect your personal information. All data is encrypted and securely stored. Access is restricted to authorized personnel only.

4. No Data Sharing
Datapoint does not share, sell, or disclose your personal information to any third party. Your data is used solely for the purposes stated in this Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies and Tracking
Datapoint does not use cookies or other tracking technologies to monitor your activity.

6. Third-Party Services
We may integrate with trusted third-party services, such as payment gateways, to process transactions. While these providers may handle your payment information, they do so independently of Datapoint. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

7. Updates to This Privacy Policy
We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. You will be notified of significant updates, and we encourage you to review this page periodically.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.
